Dear Pappa ❤️

A letter from your daughter

No matter how old I grow, I will always be your little girl.

When I was young, I thought you could solve every problem. I thought you could fix everything. And somehow... you always did.

As I grew older, I understood something important. You were never trying to be a superhero. You were simply being a father. And that is far greater.

A father who worked hard. A father who sacrificed silently. A father who worried quietly. A father who loved deeply. Even when he never said it out loud.

Thank you for every sacrifice.
Thank you for every lesson.
Thank you for every prayer.
Thank you for always believing in me.

You taught me strength. You taught me kindness. You taught me courage. You taught me how to keep moving forward.

And if there is one thing I want you to remember today...

I am proud to be your daughter. More proud than words can ever express. ❤️
"Some heroes don't wear capes. I call mine Pappa."
Love Forever,
You're Guddu ❤️
